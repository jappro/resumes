The Story

A foundation built through curiosity.

I didn't begin by choosing a single career path. I began by asking questions, taking on responsibilities, and learning how different parts of a business work together.

That curiosity led me into Web3 communities, brand communication, business operations, and data-driven problem solving. Each opportunity introduced a new perspective, but they all shared one common purpose: helping people and organizations work more effectively.

Rather than chasing titles, I've focused on building practical skills, understanding systems, and contributing wherever I can create meaningful value. Along the way, I've managed online communities, supported business operations, developed digital products, and explored how AI and data can improve everyday workflows.

Today, this collection of résumés represents more than different career paths. It reflects a single professional philosophy—approaching every challenge with curiosity, structure, ownership, and a commitment to continuous learning.
